Who is Johnny Hart?

The Genesis

My artistic odyssey began in the quiet corners of my childhood bedroom, where crayons danced across paper, and imagination knew no bounds. Those early scribbles were the seeds of a lifelong passion — a love affair with color, form, and expression.

The Freelance Frontier

As I stepped into the freelance arena, I donned my virtual beret and set sail on a sea of projects. Logos, banners, websites—I’ve crafted them all. Each client brought a unique vision, and I reveled in the challenge of translating their dreams into visual reality. From startups to established brands, my portfolio bears witness to this kaleidoscope of collaborations.
